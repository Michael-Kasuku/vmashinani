About Us

Vet Mashinani is a trusted partner in veterinary care, revolutionizing the way livestock farmers and pet owners in Kenya connect with certified veterinarians. Our platform, accessible through a mobile app, web interface, and USSD code, ensures that high-quality veterinary services are just a click or call away.

We understand that animal health is vital to the livelihoods of farmers and the well-being of pets. That's why we connect users with certified veterinarians who provide expert advice, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of animal health issues. Whether dealing with livestock diseases, routine check-ups, or emergency pet care, Vet Mashinani brings professional veterinary services directly to your doorstep.

Our Mission

To enhance the accessibility and quality of veterinary services for livestock farmers and pet owners across Kenya through innovative technology and trusted connections.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform in Africa for connecting communities to top-tier veterinary services, thereby improving animal health and the livelihoods of their owners.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all our services and interactions.
  • Innovation: We leverage technology and creative solutions to continually enhance our services.
  • Compassion: We care deeply about the well-being of animals and the prosperity of their owners.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to making veterinary care available to everyone, regardless of location.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in every aspect of our service and user experience.
